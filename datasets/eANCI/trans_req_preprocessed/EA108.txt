Useful numbers

The citizen accesses this page to take advantage of telephone numbers to contact in case of danger of any kind
The system responds allowing the visualization of a descriptive card with the telephone numbers of the bodies dealing with the 'citizen's inclumeness itself
The citizen accessing this screen can also view standard tips and procedures divided by categories about the correct communication between him and the interlocutor who was call center in a specific municipal body
The system responds with an emergency support screen corresponding to the service that the citizen decides to contact
