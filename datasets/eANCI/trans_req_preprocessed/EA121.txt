The citizen click on the button to access the taxes online tax area
The system displays the list of taxes that can be paid online
The citizen selects the section dedicated to the municipal property tax and to the relative payment
The system redirects the citizen towards the necessary section showing the form of the relative fee and the relative amount to be paid
The citizen confirms the correctness of the information and starts the transaction by selecting the preferred payment method
The system makes the transaction and show a message of payment
