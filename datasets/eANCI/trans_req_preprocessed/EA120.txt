Fine dispute

The citizen clicks on the button to access the online fines management functionality
The system redirects the citizen towards the desired section
The citizen clicks on the button for online payment of fines
The system makes a search for fines to the citizen present in the database for which the payment has not yet been received
The citizen selects the fine for which you want to carry out a dispute
The system shows all available information relating to the fine in addition to the form to be completed for the dispute
The citizen inserts the necessary information
The system verifies the correctness of the information forwards the request for dispute to those who to and notify everything to the citizen
